The GeekMagic SmallTV is a small cube with a 1.54" colour screen, an ESP inside, and a USB-C port. This firmware replaces the stock apps with three modes you switch between in a built-in web UI, and it updates over WiFi.

![The SmallTV running its three modes](/smalltv-mod/assets/screen.svg)

## The three modes

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Stock and crypto ticker" icon="seti:default">
    Price, change, percent change, and a sparkline for up to 8 rotating symbols. Data from Yahoo Finance with no backend, or your own webhook.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claude usage meter" icon="seti:crystal">
    An animated mascot plus your 5-hour and 7-day usage bars with reset countdowns, fed over WiFi by the clawdmeter-daemon on your PC.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plane radar" icon="rocket">
    A scope centred on your location with nearby aircraft, headings, speed vectors, and callsigns, from the free adsb.fi feed or a LAN webhook.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Two hardware variants

The same-looking cube ships with one of two chips. This firmware builds for both from one codebase. Confirm which board you have before you flash it.

<CardGrid>
  <LinkCard title="SmallTV (ESP8266)" href="/smalltv-mod/getting-started/hardware/" description="ESP-12F. Flashes over the air from the stock web UI." />
  <LinkCard title="SmallTV (ESP32-C2 / ESP8684)" href="/smalltv-mod/getting-started/hardware/" description="CH340C next to the USB-C port. Flashes over USB-C with esptool." />
</CardGrid>
